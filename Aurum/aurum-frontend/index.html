Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Refinement

Aurum was born from a simple conviction: that Italian cuisine, at its purest, is the most expressive food in the world. Founded by Chef JP Mbaga in the heart of Nairobi, we brought that conviction across the Mediterranean.

Every plate is composed with ingredients sourced from family-owned farms in Tuscany, Sardinia, and the Amalfi Coast — flown in twice weekly to ensure nothing is ever less than perfect.
